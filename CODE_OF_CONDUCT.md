# Code of Conduct

NT² Community spaces (including this repository and [Discord](https://discord.gg/MRRmbBe48)) exist for product discussion, peer help, and ecosystem contribution.

## Expected

- Be respectful; no harassment, spam, or scams
- Keep discussion relevant to NT² Vault and the ecosystem
- Protect privacy: never post real secrets or sensitive personal data

## Forbidden

- Master passwords, seed phrases, private keys, `.nt2backup` / recovery kits, or vault plaintext
- Impersonating Staff / Support / NT² Team
- Unsolicited binary downloads or phishing links
- Publishing unfixed security vulnerabilities as public Issues (use `security@nt2.me`)

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, lock, or delete content that violates this policy, and may block accounts that repeatedly put users at risk.

Questions: `support@nt2.me` (general) or `security@nt2.me` (security).
